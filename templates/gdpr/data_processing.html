🛡️ Data Processing & GDPR Compliance

ThingList is GDPR compliant. We are committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws.

GDPR Compliance Status

✓ GDPR Compliant ✓ Data Portability ✓ Right to Erasure ✓ Transparent Practices

Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data under the following legal bases as outlined in GDPR Article 6:

Data Type Legal Basis Purpose
Account Information Consent & Contract Service provision and account management
Inventory Data Contract Core functionality - storing your lists and items
Audit Logs Legitimate Interest Security and fraud prevention
Usage Analytics Legitimate Interest Service improvement and optimization
Technical Data Legitimate Interest Security and service stability

Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights over your personal data:

✓ Right of Access (Article 15)

You have the right to request and receive a copy of all personal data we hold about you. Use the "Export My Data" feature on your profile to download your data in JSON format.

How to exercise: Click "Export My Data" in your profile to download your data immediately.

✓ Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You have the right to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data. You can update your account information directly from your profile.

How to exercise: Update your account information directly from your profile page.

✓ Right to Erasure (Article 17 - "Right to be Forgotten")

You have the right to have your personal data deleted. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems.

How to exercise: Go to your profile and click "Delete Account" in the Danger Zone section. You will be asked to confirm with your password.

✓ Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format. We provide this functionality through our data export feature.

How to exercise: Click "Export My Data" to download all your information in JSON format.

✓ Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

You have the right to restrict how we process your personal data. You can do this by deleting your account or contacting us with specific restrictions.

✓ Right to Object (Article 21)

You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data. Contact us with details of your objection.

✓ Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7)

You can withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your account or contacting us.

✓ Right Not to be Subject to Automated Decision-Making (Article 22)

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling that would have a legal effect on you.

Data Processing Activities

Inventory Management

  • Data Processed: Lists, items, attachments, images
  • Duration: Until account deletion
  • Purpose: Core service functionality
  • Recipients: Authorized users you grant access to

Collaboration & Sharing

  • Data Processed: Share permissions, group memberships
  • Duration: Until share is revoked or account deleted
  • Purpose: Enable collaboration between users
  • Recipients: Other group members and shared users

Security & Fraud Prevention

  • Data Processed: Audit logs, access logs, IP addresses
  • Duration: 90 days (retention period)
  • Purpose: Detect and prevent unauthorized access
  • Recipients: Internal security team only

Service Improvement

  • Data Processed: Aggregated usage statistics
  • Duration: As long as account is active
  • Purpose: Analyze usage patterns and improve services
  • Recipients: Development team only

Data Retention Policy

Data Type Retention Period Deletion Method
Active Account Data Duration of account + 30 days after deletion Permanent deletion from database
Audit Logs 90 days Automatic purging
Session Data 30 days of inactivity Automatic expiration
Backup Copies 30 days Automatic rotation

Data Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data in transit uses SSL/TLS encryption
  • Password Security: Passwords stored using industry-standard hashing (bcrypt)
  • Access Control: Role-based access to user data
  • Authentication: Secure session management with CSRF protection
  • Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring and logging
  • Backup: Regular encrypted backups with 30-day retention
  • Incident Response: Documented procedures for security incidents

Third-Party Data Processors

We do not share your personal data with third parties. All data processing occurs on our own secure servers. We do not use:

  • Third-party analytics services
  • Marketing or advertising platforms
  • Data brokers or aggregators
  • Cloud services for personal data storage

Data Breach Response

In the event of a confirmed data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users without undue delay
  • Provide details about the breach and affected data
  • Recommend steps users can take to protect themselves
  • Report to regulatory authorities if required by law
  • Document the incident and response measures

International Data Transfers

All user data is stored and processed within the EU/EEA region. We do not transfer data outside the EU/EEA region without appropriate safeguards as required by GDPR.
