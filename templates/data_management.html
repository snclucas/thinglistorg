The export file is in JSON format and contains no database IDs, making it easy to import to other systems or restore later.

Import Data

Restore your data from a backup file

🔄 Smart Import:
The import system automatically detects duplicates using unique IDs and lets you choose whether to skip, overwrite, or merge your existing data.
Import Options:
  • Import complete account backups
  • Merge data from multiple exports
  • Choose conflict resolution per data type
  • Safe restoration with undo option
Conflict Resolution:
  • Skip: Keep your existing data
  • Overwrite: Replace with imported data
  • Merge: Combine both datasets
Import All Data
⚠️ Before Importing:
Make sure you have a current backup of your data. While import is safe (you choose how to handle conflicts), it's always good to have a backup just in case.

Clear All Your Data

Delete all lists, items, and groups while keeping your account active

⚠️ This Action is Permanent:
This will delete ALL your lists, items, groups, and associated data. Your account will remain active, but all your data will be permanently removed. Please back up your data first if needed.
What Gets Deleted:
  • All your lists and their items
  • All your groups and group memberships
  • All tags and custom fields
  • All list shares and permissions
  • All notifications related to your data
What Stays:
  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your account settings and preferences
  • Your email address
Tip: Consider exporting your data before clearing it, so you have a backup available.
Clear All Data

GDPR & Data Rights

Your right to your data

Under GDPR and other privacy regulations, you have the right to:
  • Access a copy of your personal data
  • Transfer your data to another service
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Receive data in a portable format

Privacy & Security

Your data is protected:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted)
  • No database IDs are exported (privacy protection)
  • Files are generated on-demand and not stored
  • Only you can export your own data
  • Rate limiting prevents abuse
